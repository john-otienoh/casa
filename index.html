About Us

Shaping the next generation of aviation specialists.

Welcome to our page, CASA is a student Association group at Moi University that aims to inspire the next generation of aviation specialists. The club presents to it's members a remarkable opportunity to gain invaulable insights from leading firgures in the ever evolving landscape of aviation. The club's core activities include Aviation career talks, debates, trips, team building activiies and charity works.
